Nu Te Supara Frate!

"Don't Get Upset, Brother!

Rules:

Player 1: Blue

Player 2: Red

The goal is to bring all your pieces to their respective "homes."

Roll the dice and click the piece you want to move.

There's a catch: If you want to move your piece out of it's starting position, you'll have to roll a 6 first.

And, whenever you roll a 6, you are given the chance to roll again!!

If you happen to land on an opposing piece, you will knock them back to their starting position!

REMEMBER:

You will lose your turn if:

You try to move your piece onto another of your own

You try to move your piece out of the starting position without first rolling a 6
